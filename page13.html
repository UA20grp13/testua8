Impact of Corona

corona, the epidemic that has been affecting us for several months now. It strikes the whole world and makes our actions heard. We can't leave the house without a mouth mask and hand disinfection has become our best friend. It already caused several people to lose their loved ones and people had to stay in the hospital for weeks.
Nevertheless, certain things are forgotten when it comes to the impact of corona. It has cost the lives of many people, but it has also caused many bankruptcies. In addition, people's mental health has deteriorated significantly and we have therefore sought refuge in various new pursuits, more specifically faith and nature. these two factors are seen in oblivion as a refuge, to escape from the problems for a while.
